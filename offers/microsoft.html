About Microsoft's offer

Microsoft's offer does not associated with your education background. Rather, it is tied with the level you are offered. Generall, Microsoft's offer package is pretty standard. And very interesting, for fresh graduate, Microsoft pays generous stocks.

Most likely, if you are fresh graduate, your package would look like:

  • For level 59, $95k + $45k + 10%
  • For level 60, $105k + $55k + 10%
  • For level 61, $115k + $55k + 10%

For industry hire, you are more likely to go from level 61 and above. Stock is going to be small, likely around $10k or so.

  • For level 61, $115k + $10k + 10%
  • For level 62, $125k + $10k + 10%
  • For level 63, $135k + $??? + 15%
But this package is highly depending on your background and your interview
