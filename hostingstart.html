Welcome to InRefer
InRefer.com is a place where we set up a bridge between job seekers and employees willing to refer external candidates.
Why us

InRefer.com is a place where job seekers meet with employees from various companies. We believe this would help both sides.

  • Companies prefer internal referrals because they believe in people employees know. Sometimes they offer generous bonuses to employees who refer other employees. In this observation, NYTimes recently published a report: Employers increasingly rely on internal referrals in hiring.
  • Job hunters prefer internal referrals because they believe internal referrals can give them more chance to get interviews. Sometimes they are willing to pay a price to get such referrals.
To use our site
  • For job seekers
    If you see some people can refer you and has his contact info listed, you can contact him/her directly.
    If you do not see his/her contact info, you can email to InRefer@outlook.com, and we will be glad to forward you the info.
  • For employees
    If you see some people that you want to refer and has his contact info listed, you can contact him/her directly.
    If you do not see his/her contact info, you can email to InRefer@outlook.com, and we will be glad to forward you the info.
